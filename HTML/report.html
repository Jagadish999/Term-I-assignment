Designing of Home page

Now the most difficult part for me was in designing the home page and took most of my time because I had no idea about portfolio websites. After getting idea by the portfolio website of famous web designer Jack kjeznach and some other portfolio website I decided a layout for my website.

The navigation buttons are created as 3D buttons with multiple shadows. This concept was given by a you tuber Ryan in his video “3D Text Effect in CSS/Ryan Web Tech” and with some use of his idea in code line 42 to 53, 166 to 173, 230 to 240, 282 to 292 the element looks like 3d object. This codes are not completely copied but still same idea exist here. Finally after this I decided to change some background color and some spacing and finalized my layout.
